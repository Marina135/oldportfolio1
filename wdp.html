Definition

Why We Chose WDP?

Workers Defense Project is a grassroots nonprofit organisation advocating for construction workers in Texas. They push for instrumental labor reforms WHICH keep sites safe and transparent, and ensure workers are paid fairly. They also advocate for the rights and protections of America's undocumentd workforce. In this redesign we hoped to take the simple but informative site and make it more appealing and functional.

Problem Statement

The Workers Defense Project website was designed to inform potential volunteers and donors about the work of the WDP, and to encourage those with the means to do so to get involved with the oganization. In our research, we noticed that certain elements of the websiteâ€™s design - namely its navigation and several page layouts - hinder users from exploring the site and learning more about WDP and its resources. How might we redesign these elements so that users can easily access the information they need?
