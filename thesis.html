Senior Thesis


Description: During my final semester in college I had to work on my Senior Thesis. For this I came up with my own fictional company called "By Heart", which sells products that has Pakistani designs. This was one of my favorite projects to work on because I was able to showcase my Pakistani background and stylist influences. The subjects in each designs were chosen based on my personal memories from Pakistan

Badshahi Mosque
This canvas portrays the Badshahi Mosque which is located in Lahore, Pakistan. Using Adobe Illustrator, I traced the outline of the mosque from a photo and then filled the image with colors that were harmonious to the brand of the company
Qawali
This canvas portrays a Qawali, which is a musical performance of Sufi Muslim Poetry. The people and the intruments in this image were traced from an picture I obtained from the internet. I added colors from my selected color palette.
Makai Wala
This canvas portrays a cart of a 'Makai Wala' or a corn seller. This was created using Adobe Illustrator. The background pattern was inspired by a wall tile design found in Pakistan.
Pakistani Truck Art
This is a sticker of a Pakistani truck. These trucks function the same as a bus or a shuttle. In Pakistan, it is quite common to see these trucks decorated with bright colors and interesting patterns.
Rickshaw
This is a sticker of a Rickshaw, a three wheeled vehicle in Pakistan used for taxi services. The colors and patterns on the vehicle is used to protray the mosaic type of artwork commonly found in Pakistan. This was created using Adobe Illustrator.
Cricket Bat
This is a sticker of a cricket bat. Cricket is a popular sport in Pakistan. The design was created using Adobe Illustrator.
Rooh Afza
This is a sticker of Rooh Afza. Rooh Afza is a sweet rose scented syrup used for beverages. It is traditionally served during Iftar in the month of Ramadan. The design was created using Adobe Illustrator.
Mango
This is a sticker design of a mango, is fruit that is common in Pakistan. This was created using Adobe Illustrator.
